A Crypt Guard is the reborn soul of a long-dead hero that has arisen from his grave and been trapped in the body of a sacrificed young adept of the K'leth cult. No matter if it was a soul of a valiant knight or a ruthless warlord, once his soul is trapped in the body of the K'leth's cult adept, it would be bound to serve evil. Some say that the barbarian tradition of burning of passed away warlords and chiefs was created in order to save their souls from this dark fate.
